Lockscreen Image Generator

How to use: In the text box above, put a list of phrases that you want displayed. This could be positive affirmations, daily routine reminders, or betrayals by an ex-partner.

Select the width and height based on your device screen resolution. Use the Y-axis position and font size to position the text. Select your colors, and an optional background image.

Generate the images and save them to your device. Use either built-in software or an app that allows you to rotate through the lockscreen photos you generated.
